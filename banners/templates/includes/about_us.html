About Us

We are here to create the most exciting taste experiences for you. For this, we are constantly on the lookout for the most interesting and diverse flavor profiles from all over the world.

We source our coffee seasonally, which means we only buy the right amount of the annual harvest to meet seasonal demand and to ensure that we always have the freshest and most aromatic product for you.

We prefer to buy our coffee directly from the local producers in order to ensure direct quality control of the processing methods and on the other hand to guarantee that premium prices are paid for higher quality coffees, which contribute significantly to the sustainability and livelihood of the farmers.

Fairtrade certificates are a good start, but they are far from meeting our expectations of truly fair coffee. That's why we don't focus on traditional Fairtrade prices, but on quality. We believe that it is fair to pay a higher price for high-quality coffee, which is on average four times the Fairtrade price for our Standard Specialty Coffees.

We store our seasonally sourced green coffee beans in our custom-made, air-conditioned bean humidor (Cooling Strorage) to keep all aromas as fresh as possible until roasting and thus provide you with the freshest and most aromatic coffee.

We roast for sweetness and test until we have found the ideal roasting profile for each coffee and highlight the individual potential of each coffee. We roast on one of the most innovative and environmentally friendly roasters of the Loring brand. For our Competition Coffees (Premium Picks) we roast in small batches on a Stronghold roaster.

As one of the most innovative roasters in Europe, we use two so-called color sorters to sort our coffee in order to achieve the clearest possible result in the cup. The first sorter is used before roasting and sorts out the finest particles from our high-quality green coffee. After roasting, the coffee passes through five pitters and is then sorted by another color sorter, which sorts out beans that are too light and too dark.
